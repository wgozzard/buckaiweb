About Us

Our Roots in Columbus

At the heart of Columbus, Ohio, we are revolutionizing how small businesses interact with their customers online. We introduce a dynamic chatbot solution designed specifically for small businesses.

Design: Your Vision, Brought to Life

At our chat bot agency, we understand that every business is unique. That's why we start by listening to your needs, goals, and vision for your ideal chat bot. Our team of experienced designers will work closely with you to craft a custom decision tree that aligns perfectly with your brand and engages your target audience. From incorporating eye-catching visuals to seamlessly capturing valuable lead information, we'll ensure your bot is designed to exceed your expectations and drive results.

Deploy: Effortless Integration, Instant Results

Once you've approved your custom-designed chat bot, it's time to bring it to life. Our streamlined deployment process takes the stress out of integration. We'll provide you with a few simple lines of code that can be easily added to your website. If you need assistance, our tech-savvy team is always ready to lend a helping hand. With our expertise, your chat bot will be up and running in no time, engaging your visitors and generating leads from day one.

Distribute: Actionable Insights

We provide comprehensive engagement reports tailored to your preferences. Whether you need weekly, monthly, or quarterly insights, we've got you covered. But we don't stop there. Our innovative integration with Google Sheets allows us to seamlessly inject chat bot interactions into your spreadsheet, giving you real-time access to valuable data. With our detailed reporting and seamless data integration, you'll have the tools you need to measure success, refine your strategy, and grow your business like never before.
